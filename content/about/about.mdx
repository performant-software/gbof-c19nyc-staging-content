To recover the history of New York City's nineteenth-century Latina/o press, students at Pace University have been locating the forgotten sites of the publications that once resided there and beginning to re-imagine the ways in which writers and
editors of the city's Spanish- and English-languages publishing communities might have interacted with each other.
